Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Gist — Summarize & Translate ("the extension") is a browser extension that summarizes and translates web page content using AI. This policy explains exactly what the extension does and does not do with your data.

No server, no data collection

The developer operates no server and collects no personal data. The extension has no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising. We never see your API key, your settings, the pages you visit, or the text you summarize or translate.

Your API key and settings

The extension uses your own OpenAI or Anthropic API key. Your API key and preferences (provider and model) are stored only in your browser via chrome.storage and are never transmitted to the developer. They remain on your device(s) and can be removed at any time by clearing the extension's settings or uninstalling it.

What happens to page text

When — and only when — you click "Summarize" or "Translate" (or use the right-click menu), the extension reads the text of the current page or your selected text and sends it directly from your browser to the AI provider you chose (OpenAI or Anthropic), authenticated with your own API key. This is required to generate the summary or translation.

That text is processed by the AI provider under their privacy policy and terms:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Privacy Policy

The extension only contacts api.openai.com and api.anthropic.com. It contacts no other servers.

Permissions

  • activeTab / scripting — read the current tab's text only at the moment you click, to summarize or translate it.
  • storage — save your API key and settings in your browser.
  • contextMenus — provide the right-click "Summarize / Translate selection" items.
  • host access to api.openai.com and api.anthropic.com — send your text to the AI provider using your key.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with anyone. The only transmission of your text is the request you trigger, sent directly from your browser to your chosen AI provider.
